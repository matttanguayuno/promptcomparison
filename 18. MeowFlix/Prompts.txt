Design a 'Netflix for Cats' app interface where users can subscribe to live streams of cats around the world, rate their favorite felines, and send virtual treats. The vibe should be playful but sleek—think Tinder meets Animal Planet with a dark mode aesthetic. Use these guidelines: Must-Have Elements: 1. Hero Section: - A looping video placeholder (or animated gradient) of a cat yawning dramatically. - Tagline: 'Unlimited Cats. Zero Disappointments.' - CTA: 'Start Your Free Trial (Humans Only)' 2. Live Stream Grid: - Show 3-5 'trending' cat streams with: - Circular thumbnails (like Instagram Stories) - Real-time viewer counts - A floating heart button to 'send treats' 3. Featured Cat Profile: - A horizontally scrollable card for 'Cat of the Hour' with: - Name, age, breed - A 5-star rating system (paw icons, not stars) - Bio: 'Loves naps, hates Mondays. 12k treat points this week!' 4. Dark Mode Aesthetic - Color Palette: - Background: #121212 (near-black) - Text: #FFFFFF (pure white) with #E0E0E0 for subtitles - Accents: #FF9F1C (vibrant orange) and #A569BD (purple for VIP features) - Use glow effects on interactive elements (buttons, icons). 5. Micro-Interactions: - Hover effects on cards (slight scale-up + shadow). - A bouncing animation on the 'Send Treat' button.
