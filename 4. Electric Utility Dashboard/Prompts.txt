UNO


Generate an Electric Utility operations dashboard page.
Requirements:
- KPI row (4 items): demand, supply, reserve, frequency
- Each KPI shows a label and a value inside a card-like container
- A scrollable list of facilities (ListView)
- A right-hand rail containing alerts
- Use SafeArea + AutoLayout
