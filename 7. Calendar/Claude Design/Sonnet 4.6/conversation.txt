Here's your 2025 Advent Calendar! 🎄

Here's what's in it:

25 doors in a varied mosaic grid — small 1×1 squares up to large 3×2 feature tiles (days 15, 25)
Flip animation on click reveals a festive icon + message behind each door
Pop-up modal with the full day's surprise after flipping
Falling snow drifting across the deep navy backdrop
Progress bar tracking how many doors you've opened
localStorage persistence — your opened doors survive a refresh
Toggle Tweaks from the toolbar to control snow, the header glow, and the progress counter — or reset all doors to start fresh.
